Sound Circuits

Sound Circuits is a local Seattle meetup where you come and work on DIY music projects. It happens every other Wednesday, 5pm - 9pm.

Example Projects

  • soldering together a synthesizer kit
  • building an effects pedal
  • fixing or refurbishing a broken piece of music equipment
  • building a software Arduino instrument

We want you to:

  • learn new techniques
  • have access to help and tools
  • be able to bounce ideas off other like-minded people
